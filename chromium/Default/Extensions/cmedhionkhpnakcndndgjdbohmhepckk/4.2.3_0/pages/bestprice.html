About Best Price

Best Price is a new extension featured by Adblock for Youtube™.

The feature helps you save money by showing your alternative offers to the product you're currently searching for. We show you:

  • The lowest price searches for your products
  • Relevant coupons and offers
  • No ads! Just relevant information.

We hope you enjoy it!

You can always enable or disable this feature with a click on the Adblock for Youtube™ button in the upper right corner!
